F.A.Q


Q: Will I be contributing to charity after I exit the page?


Charity Mine will not use your computer’s unused CPU power after you close the webpage. So, if you want to continue contributing to charity, simply leave the Charity Mine tab open in your browser while you work on other things.


Q: How can I be certain that the money I’m helping generate is being donated?


Every charitable donation that The Charity Mine makes will be posted on the site and our social media pages. We plan on making our initial donation when we raise our first fifty dollars. Itemized donation receipts will be posted under the, “Donation Receipts” tab on the webpage.


Q: Will my electricity bill be effected by running this site?


No. The amount of power that The Charity Mine is using to mine Monero (XMR), is negligible. The amount of money that you’re helping contribute is far more significant than the cost of donating your unused CPU power.
