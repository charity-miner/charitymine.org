About Us


Mission:


Our mission is to provide internet users with an easily accessible platform to crowdfund money for charitable causes. Charity Mine is dedicated to helping ordinary internet users do extraordinary good.


Vision:


We aim to create and sustain an easily accessible and massively effective charity crowd-funding platform. We envision a society where all humans have equal ability to contribute to causes that effect our shared planet and social capital.


How it started:


One evening, Ryan Visek was sitting on the front porch of his college rental in Chico, CA. He was tired of doing tedious school assignments that only benefited himself. Ryan had recently been learning about cryptocurrency and its applications, and it lead him to do more research about the opportunities to do good with it.
